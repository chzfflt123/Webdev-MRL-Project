About

Disease Detector is a program for doctors and professionals in the field to assist in identifing possible underlying diseases based on the symptoms and conditions of the patient. There is a list of symptoms which are checked by the doctor and professionals. Patient information, their symptoms, and their possible underlying diseases are then saved onto a database for future reference.
